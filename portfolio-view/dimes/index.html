Dimes: To Teach Your Child About Money

  • « Previous post
  • Date:August, 2014

Reading this book is a simple and fun activity to engage your child and teach them the importance of money in our lives. It teaches discipline, delayed gratification and how good it feels to give to those in need.

Dimes can teach your child the habits that will allow them to have a more financially secure and fulfilling life.
