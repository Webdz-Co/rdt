Chosen: An Adoption Story

  • « Previous post
  • Next Post »
  • Date:August 20, 2011

When you are adopted you are different but different special. Being an adopted child myself, I wanted to create a caring and enjoyable way for a family to explain to adopted children just how they came to be and how their coming to be might be different from other children they know or meet.

I can tell you that the “little book” that my parents read to me gave me the positive, optimistic view that I believe is my core foundation for all areas of my life.

This book also explains to the child that even though we may all look very different on the outside, there are things that each of us share that are the same.

My hope and belief is that by reading this book to your children, they will grow up knowing how special they are and that they will have the foundation for their self-esteem to aid them into their adult lives.
